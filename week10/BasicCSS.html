Space and size




Most stylesheets contain more than one rule, and most rules contain more than one declaration. The use of multiple declarations and spaces makes the stylesheet easier to be edited.



Whether or not the inclusion of a space does not affect the work effect of CSS in the browser. Similarly, unlike the XHTML, CSS is insensitive to big or small. However, there is an exception: if it is involved in working with HTML documents, the class and ID names are sensitive to big or small.
